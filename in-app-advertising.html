It's no secret that social media is filled with ads. Platforms like Facebook and Instagram were designed for users to share photos, videos & socialize but they've also become powerful marketing tools for businesses of all sizes. And as more and more businesses flock to social media to advertise their products and services, the competition for attention has intensified. So, how can you make sure your ads stand out from the crowd? how can you break the clutter of online advertising?

In-App advertisements are an alternative to the traditional social media or banners that we see on web pages. They are more interactive and engaging for the user.

The best way to advertise is to grab users' attention and also give them a good experience with it. This is where In-App ads come in. They are not intrusive, and they don't interfere with the user's experience. They also have high click rates because of their relevance to what the user is doing at that moment.

In-App Video Advertising Platform

In-app advertising is becoming one of the most popular options for mobile marketers. In-app ad spending is expected to grow by 52 percent this year, reaching $11.5 billion, according to a study by App Annie and IDC. That growth rate is more than twice as fast as ad spending on other forms of digital advertising, such as desktop or mobile web ads.

It is also a form of advertising that occurs when a user is engaged in a mobile app. It can be done through display ads, video ads, or interstitials as there are many apps that can provide options like in-app location advertising, in-app video advertising platform, in-app advertising market etc

Interstitials are the most popular form of in-app advertising. They are full-screen ads that cover the entire screen of an app, and they usually occur when a user is trying to do something, such as open an app, finish a task within an app, or close an app.

Mobile in-app advertising

Display ads are the most common type of in-app ad. They are small units that are placed within an app. Video ads are more engaging and effective than static banners because they offer a more immersive experience for the user. Video ads can also be used to promote your products or drive traffic to your website to display your entire range of products.

In-app Advertising also has several other benefits like :

  • It's more targeted than other forms of mobile advertising because it can be delivered to specific audiences through precise targeting
  • It's more relevant because it only appears when the user is engaged with the app and can be customized to suit their preferences.
  • It has a higher conversion rate than other forms of mobile advertising as it's more likely to catch users' attention.
